About Me. A bit of background.

Hi, I’m Colin, a web developer from Essex. I have been developing websites for around 10 years, starting out from necessity producing websites for my band. I have since made the transition into a freelance developer, producing websites for clients since late 2013. This site displays my work since then and my current skill set. I can design and build websites as well as develop their functionality. I can also construct database driven solutions and e-commerce shops.

I currently work full time for a bank along side my freelance work and my goal is to gain further experience by attaining a permanent role as a web developer. If you would like to work with me on a project, or feel I could fit in working for your business, please get in touch. I am self taught and have a passion to learn, spending any free time keeping up to date with the latest technologies and trends.

Skills. What I can do.

Understand.

I will work with you to fully understand the needs of you, your website and your business. We will work together to determine what it is that your website needs to do, and how your online solution will provide it. We will identify the goals of your website and how it is to be used. I will personally ensure your site is the platform you need to achieve your goals. Using Analytical Tools you will have an ongoing understanding of how your website is performing, and I am personally available to provide advice and assistance, to optimise that performance.

Coding Languages.

My varied skill set caters for carefully designed, structured websites that provide a fluid and interactive user experience across all devices, with unlimited database driven possibilities. I have experience of the following coding languages, HTML, CSS, jQuery, JavaScript, PHP and MySQL.

Design.

With a keen eye for detail and a strong knowledge of the Adobe Design Package; Photoshop and Illustrator, I am able to design your website from scratch. I will work with you to construct wire frame models of what your website will look like at a basic level. Full graphical mock ups will then be produced and refined until you are happy. Your website will then be built to be an exact replica of your chosen design. I know how important it is to capture your brand and identity when creating your website. As such I will take time to understand your current brand (or what you want your brand to be) and ensure it is well represented. I have a personal approach and am happy to work with you to design your site together, or provide bespoke work to a brief or alternatively, convert an existing design you have into a website.

Marketing.

I am passionate about making your website really work for you. If required I will work with you after the site is live to ensure we can maximise exposure to your company, products and services through online marketing. I have a very strong sales background leading to experience as a successful Sales Manager for 5 years. With my knowledge of Inbound Marketing, Outbound Marketing and Social Media Marketing, I will help you work the crowd to get your business to the masses. Metrics and statistical analysis will be used to show how your reach and influence is growing. When compared to your marketing efforts, these metrics will show you the Return On Investment you are receiving through your chosen methods, which will enable you to build up an effective and manageable Marketing Strategy for your business.

Search Engine Optimisation.

It is important to remember that a website not only needs to work well, look great and convey the right message but also needs to be found. People need to notice your company when searching the internet for a business like yours. With an understanding of how to stand out among your competition and rank highly on search engines, your website will be developed to ensure search engines know where to find you. This means users will be able to find your company and your content as easily as possible. More people on your site means more traffic, which is vital when you begin to factor in conversions towards your goals.

UX - User Experience.

Not only is it important that your users find you and that your product and services are displayed in the right light but users must enjoy their experience while they are on your site. I am invested in providing intuitive designs that provide great usability and interaction. This includes catering for those who may be visually impaired and compatibility across devices. Your website will be fully responsive ensuring it looks great and is user friendly, regardless of whether it is viewed on a phone, laptop, tablet, desktop computer or screen reader - on all major browsers.

Content Management Systems. Keeping Up To Date.

To ensure your website can adapt as a business should, your solution can either be swiftly updated and added to as and when you require with a simple phone call or email; or alternatively, a Content Management System (CMS) can be built into your site so you can update your content instantly and effortlessly your self.

Support.

I am a patient problem solver with pride in my work and absolute respect for you as a client. If anything goes wrong or you need advice or help with anything I will endeavor to be at hand as soon as possible to assist you. As part of an ongoing relationship, I will strive to help you with any technical issues you may encounter with any part of my service, and assist you with any further work you may require.
